Ground truth

An experiment in grounded conversation with ancient texts. Each interlocutor is built on the primary sources themselves — it reads them, cites them, and will not invent. The aim is to ground-truth spiritual thinking and feeling: to let each tradition speak in its own irreducible terms, rather than dissolve it into paraphrase.
